PokéPlumbers: Gotta Fix 'Em All!

Ever wondered what happens when Pokémon trainers retire? They become PokéPlumbers, of course! This company specializes in fixing plumbing issues caused by mischievous or overenthusiastic Pokémon.

Services Offered

Pikachu Power Surge Protection:

Protect your home from sudden electrical surges caused by Pikachu's Thunderbolt attacks.

Jigglypuff Pipe Repair:

Fixing clogged pipes caused by Jigglypuff's excessive singing. We'll unclog those pipes and make sure your bathroom doesn't turn into a concert hall.

Snorlax Drain Clearing:

Clearing blocked drains caused by Snorlax's appetite. No drain is too big or too clogged for our team to handle.

Squirtle Squad Water Damage Restoration:

When the Squirtle Squad gets a little too rowdy, we're here to clean up the mess and restore your home to its former glory.

Charmander Chimney Cleaning:

Keeping your chimney free of soot and debris caused by Charmander's tail flame. Safety first, even in the Pokémon world!

Meowth Mischief Prevention:

Installing anti-Meowth devices to prevent these sneaky Pokémon from causing trouble with your plumbing fixtures.
